GuessTheCoaster is a Discord Bot where Users have to Guess the Name of a Roller Coaster from a large selection of coaster images. Correct answers reward Credits and Completion which can be viewed on a User Profile or on Local/Global Leadboards. The Bot is coded in JavaScript with help from Nodejs, Discordjs, and Mongodb. Bot Commands: /about - brings up this about page. /guess - play a round of GuessTheCoaster. /profile - view the stats page of a player. /top - view the leaderboard of local players. /topall - view the leaderboard of global players.
